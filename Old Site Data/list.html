Official California Bird Lists

The CBRC maintains two lists:
The California Bird List is the official state list. A plain text version is available for easy importing into a spreadsheet.

The CBRC Review List provides a convenient listing of the species and forms the CBRC reviews.

The CBRC "Watch List" of Introduced Birds provides a listing of breeding introduced species that are not yet on the official state list, but which could be added in the future.
