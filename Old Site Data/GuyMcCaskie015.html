Honorary Presentation for Guy McCaskie

At the 2015 Election, past and current CBRC members presented a special award to Guy McCaskie who retired as Secretary after serving the committee as a member and as secretary since the committee's founding in 1970. We are pleased to honor Guy with original artwork by Keith Hansen illustrating a bird's-eye view of Red Hill Marina at the Salton Sea from the point of view of a flock of Magnificent Frigatebirds flying up from the Sea of Cortez into California. Both the birds and the location have special meaning to Guy and it is with deep gratitude that the CBRC is pleased to honor him. Although Guy has retired as Secretary, he will continue as a valued advisor to the CBRC.
