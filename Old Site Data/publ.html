CALIFORNIA BIRD RECORDS COMMITTEE

Publications List

The following publications were authored, or coauthored, by members of the California Bird Records Committee (CBRC), and are relevant to various Committee purposes. Authors or coauthors were members of the CBRC at, or near, the time of publication. The list is intended to be flexible enough that previous publications can be added as new individuals join the CBRC, or as additional purposes are adopted. General publications that cover many species (e.g., regional avifaunas) are not included.
