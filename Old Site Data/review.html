Review List of the

Western Field Ornithologists’
California Bird Records Committee

14 August 2009

The sequence of birds on this list has been updated to follow the 7th edition of the A.O.U Check-List of North American Birds, and supplements.

The California Bird Records Committee solicits information on all occurrences in California of the 183 species on this Review List, as well as species unrecorded in California. In general, review species average four or fewer occurrences per year in California, and have been recorded fewer than 100 times. Documentation should be sent to Guy McCaskie, California Bird Records Committee, P.O. Box 275, Imperial Beach, CA 91933-0275 (or by e-mail to guymcc@pacbell.net). Documentation concerning the potential establishment of exotic species not currently on the state list is also sought.

Legend
For species not supported by specimens (100 species):
P - At least one record supported by identifiable photograph (98 species)
V - At least one record supported by identifiable videotape (20 species)
T - At least one record supported by identifiable taped vocalization (6 species)
S - Supported only by sight records (2 species)
