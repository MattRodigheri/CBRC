Online Version

updated 9 July 2015

We are pleased that almost all known errors in the print version have been corrected in this electronic version. However four errors from the print version remain uncorrected. They are: Main List: Move Ringed Storm-Petrel to follow Fork-tailed Storm-Petrel. Appendix H: American Woodcock. Last sentence, change "...remote oases in the southern Mojave Desert...." to "...remote oases in the southern Mojave and adjacent Colorado Deserts." We hope that these errors will be corrected soon.
